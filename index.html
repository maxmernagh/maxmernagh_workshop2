THE MAPLE LEAFS
RECIPE FOR SUCCESS

How the Toronto Maple Leafs can END their 53 year Stanley Cup drought in 2021.

The last time the Maple Leafs hoisted Lord Stanley’s Cup was in 1967. This was when the NHL was still a 6 team league and many goalies still didn’t wear goalie masks. Toronto was clear underdogs to win the Stanley Cup, but after upsetting the Chicago Blackhawks in Round 1 and the Montreal Canadians in the Finals in the 1967 NHL Playoffs, the Toronto Maple Leafs brought glory back to the city of Toronto for the 13th time, and the last time. In 2021, however, the Maple Leafs have all the ingredients necessary to bring glory back to the city of Toronto.


Here’s how the boys in blue can be Stanley Cup Champions for the 14th time at the end of the 2021 NHL season.


  1. STAY HEALTHY

    2. Injuries are common in the NHL. The leafs need to have everyone healthy to continue to improve.


  2. STAR PLAYERS DOMINATE

    3. To keep winning, Matthews and Marner need to keep being productive every game.


  3. BUILD FROM SUCCESS

    4. All 4 lines need to apply pressure to their opponent every shift.


  4. STRONG DEFENSE, FAST OFFENCE

    5. Limit your opponents scoring chances, and get pucks on net.


  5. POWERPLAY SUCCESS

    6. Take advantage of other teams mistakes.

  1. MENTAL TOUGHNESS FROM GOALIES

    2. Andersen, Hutchison, and Campbell have to stay confident and composed.


  2. FINISH FIRST IN THE NORTH

    3. Finishing with the 1st seed in the North Division is key to the Leafs Playoff success.


  3. EFFECTIVE LEADERSHIP

    4. Share feedback and have strong group focus.


  4. HIGH PUCK POSSESSION

    5. Take care of the puck and limit the opponents touches.


  5. GET LUCKY

    6. Having luck on your side is always a bonus.




Hopefully the Leafs don't choke in the Playoffs this year like they have in recent years...
